What does 'Assured Purchase' stands for?
'Assured Purchase' is a buyer-centric guarantee from NowFloats focused on aspects of quality, reliability and Purchase protection. Assured products have high quality standards and delivered hassle free. Buyers can be assured that their money is safe with NowFloats. In case a product does not match buyer's expectations as described while purchasing, they can get in touch with our support team and raise a request to refund your money. The amount paid for a product will be transferred to the seller only after the buyer is satisfied with the product.
